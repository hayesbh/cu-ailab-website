The PhD in Computer Science at CU Boulder is a premier research program dedicated to training the next generation of thought leaders in artificial intelligence and computing. Students work side-by-side with world-renowned faculty on projects that push the boundaries of what's possible.

Our doctoral candidates are fully funded and immersed in a collaborative environment that values innovation, ethical responsibility, and technical excellence. Graduates go on to secure tenure-track positions at top universities and research roles at leading tech companies.
